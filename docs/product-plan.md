# TutorMate Product Plan

## Product Name

TutorMate

## Product Type

Web platform for online English tutors.

## Main Idea

TutorMate helps online English tutors manage students, lessons, attendance, homework, progress, timesheets, and AI-generated lesson materials in one place.

## Target Users

The first version is designed for independent online English tutors.

Future users may include:

- language centers
- IELTS tutors
- school teachers
- tutors of other subjects

## Problem Statement

Online English tutors often use many separate tools to manage teaching work. For example, Google Classroom may be used for materials, WhatsApp for communication, Word for timesheets, and notebooks for progress tracking.

This process is inconvenient because:

- students do not always check Google Classroom
- lesson tracking is manual
- timesheets take time to prepare
- homework and attendance are difficult to track together
- student progress is not clearly visible
- tutors spend a lot of time preparing lesson materials
- feedback messages are written manually after lessons

## Solution

TutorMate will provide a single dashboard where tutors can manage their teaching process from lesson planning to post-lesson feedback.

The platform will allow tutors to:

- add and manage students
- record lessons
- track attendance
- assign homework
- write post-lesson comments
- generate formatted feedback messages
- create monthly timesheets
- track student progress
- use AI to generate lesson plans and speaking activities

## MVP Scope

The MVP will focus only on English tutors.

The first version will include:

1. Tutor account
2. Student profiles
3. Lesson records
4. Attendance tracking
5. Homework tracking
6. Post-lesson feedback
7. Timesheet generation
8. Progress tracking
9. AI lesson assistant

## Unique Feature

The main unique feature is the AI Lesson Assistant.

The tutor can enter:

- student age
- level
- book
- grammar topic
- vocabulary topic
- lesson duration
- lesson goal

The system will generate:

- warm-up activity
- grammar explanation
- examples
- speaking questions
- role-play
- homework
- mini quiz

## Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if:

- a tutor can create an account
- a tutor can add students
- a tutor can record lessons
- a tutor can track attendance and homework
- a tutor can generate a monthly timesheet
- a tutor can create post-lesson feedback
- a tutor can generate AI lesson materials
- the project is deployed online
- the GitHub repository is clean and documented
